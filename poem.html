Happy Birthday!

Six years apart, yet close from the start,
My cousin sister, you've captured my heart.
I remember the first time I held you tight,
A six-year-old boy, in pure delight.

You were my little companion, my joy every day,
We'd laugh and we'd play, come what may.
Your beauty shines, dusky skin and brown eyes,
Gleaming brightly under sunny skies.

Small and adorable, with a heart so pure,
Together, we'd find adventures to explore.
Though just cousins, you felt like my own,
In your home, I found my second zone.

Through holidays spent side by side,
In your company, I found my pride.
Misunderstandings and disputes may have come,
But our bond remains strong, forever one.

You are my best friend, my sister true,
And on this special day, I celebrate you.
Happy 17th birthday, may joy fill your days,
May your future be bright in countless ways.
